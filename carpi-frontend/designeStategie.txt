# Design Strategy: Carpi Shop (Premium Dark Theme)

This document outlines the design and styling strategy used in the Carpi Shop project to maintain consistency in future projects.

## 1. Color Palette (Dark & Gold Theme)
The project follows a "Premium Dark" aesthetic, using deep blacks and high-contrast gold accents.

- **Backgrounds:**
  - Main Background: `#0D0D0D` (Pure dark)
  - Surface Dark: `#121212` (Cards and sections)
  - Surface Light: `#1A1A1A` (Inputs and active elements)
- **Accents:**
  - Primary Gold: `#D4A017`
  - Gold Gradient: `linear-gradient(135deg, #D4A017, #B8860B)`
  - Muted Gold: `#8B6508`
- **Text:**
  - Primary: `#FFFFFF` (Pure white)
  - Secondary/Muted: `#B3B3B3` (Gray)
- **Borders:**
  - Dark Border: `#2C2C2E`

## 2. Typography
- **Primary Font:** 'Inter' or 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (fallback: sans-serif).
- **Headings:** Bold (700+), letter-spacing `0.02em` for a modern, clean look.
- **Logo:** Heavy weight (900), gradient text-clip, negative letter-spacing (`-1px` to `-2px`) for a compact, brand-forward feel.

## 3. Core UI Components & Styling Patterns

### Cards & Grid
- **Border Radius:** `12px` (Standard Card) / `30px` (Pills/Buttons).
- **Hover Effects:**
  - Subtle lift: `transform: translateY(-8px) scale(1.02)`.
  - Border highlight: `border-color: var(--primary-gold)`.
  - Deep shadows: `box-shadow: 0 12px 40px rgba(0,0,0,0.6)`.
- **Media Aspect Ratio:** `aspect-ratio: 1` (square) for product thumbnails.

### Navigation & Headers
- **Sticky Headers:** Search and category bars remain fixed while scrolling content.
- **Navbar Glassmorphism:** Uses `linear-gradient(180deg, rgba(0,0,0,0.7) 10%, transparent 100%)` initially, turning solid `#0D0D0D` on scroll.
- **Bottom Navigation:** Mobile-friendly bottom bar for quick access.

### Buttons & Inputs
- **Pills:** Category buttons use `border-radius: 30px`.
- **Inputs:** Search bars use `surface-light` background with subtle borders and inner icons.
- **Welcome Buttons:** 
  - Primary (Gold Gradient) for high-intent actions (Sign up).
  - Secondary (Black with thin border) for low-intent (Login).

## 4. Visual Effects
- **Gradients:** Extensive use of gradients for overlays to ensure text readability over images.
- **Glassmorphism:** Use of `backdrop-filter: blur(4px)` on tags and overlays.
- **Transitions:** Smooth bezier transitions: `all 0.3s cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)`.

## 5. Layout Strategy
- **Mobile-First:** Responsive grids (`repeat(auto-fill, minmax(220px, 1fr))`) that collapse to 2 columns on mobile.
- **Horizontal Scrolling:** Used for categories and promoted items to maximize vertical space.
- **Standard Padding:** `4vw` for desktop, `24px` for mobile.

## 6. Libraries Used
- **Lucide React:** For consistent, thin-stroke iconography.
- **Framer Motion:** For page transitions, `AnimatePresence` triggers, and entrance animations (`initial={{ opacity: 0 }}`).
